Quality Education

Education is an important aspect of life: it is required to develop a rational person who can help shape the world, it is a priority for anyone pursuing an honorable life, and it is the foundation for all past, present, and future generations.

Nevertheless, the modern educational system is inconsistent around the world due to several constraints, such as resources and money, as well as how education is organized and administered to students.

That is why UNESCO stepped in and included "Quality Education" as the fourth Sustainable Development Goal ( global goals designed to be a "blueprint to achieve a better and more sustainable future for all"), exhibiting the importance of education in achieving a sustainable future for all of us and giving the priority for solving this problem .

So We decided to contribute to "Quality Education" for the same reason. However, because a group of ambitious students (like us) will not be able to solve this problem overnight, we have decided to raise awareness, assist some children, and establish this foundation (the website) that will potentially contribute to doing so in the future.

Surprisingly, assisting some students was not difficult for us, but rather enjoyable. Initially, we worked with a public school and provided free classes in which we explained some fundamentals of physics as well as lab instructions to its lovely students.

Then we prepared our materials (presentations, instruments, and so on), and finally we taught the class, both physically and via video conferencing - because some of us were unable to be physically present.

In many cases, you can change the trajectory of a struggling child by improving everything from nutrition to life skills.
